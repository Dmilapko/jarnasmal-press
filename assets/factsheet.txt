One-liner
---------
A bullet hell where Wi-Fi is your lifeline. Your blaster needs a live radio
link to your ground station — build a mesh network of repeaters, or face the
horde with nothing.

Description
-----------
Your signal is your survival. Jarnasmal is a bullet hell built on one idea:
your high-tech blaster requires a live radio connection to your ground
station — and every wall, corridor, and cavern in the underground facility
blocks your signal.

Strong connection: endless ammunition, maximum damage, maxed-out skills.
Weak connection: your gun stalls, abilities degrade, explosions shrink.
No connection: your blaster is dead, and the horde is still coming.

You can't just run and gun — you plan. Place signal repeaters to forge a path
through the depths, calculate tech-tree combinations, push radio performance
with higher power, wall penetration, and directional antennas — while dodging
thousands of projectiles at 1000-to-1 odds.

Key features
------------
- Signal = survival: connection strength directly drives your firepower
- Mesh-network building: every wall matters; repeater placement is your build
- Deep repeater tech tree: multiply effects, boost power, punch through walls
- Overwhelming odds: wall-climbers, mine layers, room floods, heavy tanks
- Hand-drawn art: every frame inked by hand, 100% human-made

In the demo

5 tutorial missions plus ~20 minutes of gameplay in the Underground Temple
biome.
