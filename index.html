About the Game

Your signal is your survival. Jarnasmal is a bullet hell built on one idea: your high-tech blaster requires a live radio connection to your ground station — and every wall, corridor, and cavern in the underground facility blocks your signal.

Strong connection means endless ammunition, maximum damage, maxed-out skills. Weak connection and your gun stalls, your abilities degrade, your explosions shrink. No connection — your blaster is dead, and the horde is still coming.

You can't just run and gun. You plan: placing signal repeaters to forge a path through the depths, calculating tech-tree combinations, pushing radio performance with higher power, wall penetration, and directional antennas — while dodging thousands of projectiles at 1000-to-1 odds.

  • Signal = survival — connection strength directly drives your firepower
  • Mesh-network building — every wall matters; repeater placement is your build
  • Deep repeater tech tree — multiply effects, boost power, punch through walls
  • Overwhelming odds — wall-climbers, mine layers, room floods, heavy tanks
  • Hand-drawn art — every frame inked by hand, 100% human-made
